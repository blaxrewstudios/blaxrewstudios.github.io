We're a small team producing Software, Games, Music, Videos, Movies and other stuff. Many software projects are open source and available on github. If you want to learn more about our projects click here.

Careers

If you think you are the right one and want to apply fill in the form below or send an email to blaxrew.studios@gmail.com with the subject "Application" to apply. We are not training you. When you apply and we accept, we are going to test if you fit to the rest of the team. This is an unpaid job, that means you will not gain any money at the moment if you are part of the team.
