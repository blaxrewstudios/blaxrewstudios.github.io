Driving Simulator Trailer

At the moment we do not have a trailer for the game. We used one of Blackture's Videos as placeholder. Trailer is coming soon.

A Unity 3D Game which is using URP. The driving simulator is made to train to drive with a trailer. Drive using different perspectives and train on specific places without to damage anything in real-life. Try to complete missions without damaging or getting damaged by anything. Decide wether you want to drive at night or daytime. Just choose a car, a trailer and get to it with or without a steering wheel controller. (TMX Pro compatibility tested) 

The first release canditate of the alpha is out. Go and have fun with it.

In the future there will be more trailers to drive with. Scores may get tracked per user (global or local)
