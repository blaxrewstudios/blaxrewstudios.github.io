Thig is a singleplayer FPS controlled story adventure game.

You start as boy aged around 16. You get to know the that boy has got blurred views. He called those views "visions" or "additions to the world". In those "visions" he sees images of destruction and elimination. His so called addition shows shadows. Suddenly the vision are ordering themselves, getting true.

Get the game as we release it if you want to know more about the story. Subscribe to the newsletter to get those information via mail.

The game is in progress the story is not finished at the moment. Without the story finished we can not start designing it.

The future plans are the game itself.
