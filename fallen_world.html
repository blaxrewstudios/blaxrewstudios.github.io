Thig is a singleplayer first person controlled story adventure game.

About:
In the region called Nojocron a giant, humanoid, robotic something appear. Three days after the Initiative of Nojocron (ION) had attempted to study the robotic somethings, the robotic somethings had initiated an annihilation of all Nojocron's inhabitants. Throughout the story you will learn many secrets of Nojocron, from its ancient origins to the present day, helping you in your quests.

Get the game as we release it if you want to know more about the story. Subscribe to the newsletter to get those information via mail.

The game is in progress the story is not finished at the moment. Without the story finished we can not start designing it.

The future plans are the game itself.
